PURPOSE:
- Calculates the volume and mass of different size dumbbells made of different metals.

TASKS:
- Calculate the volume of the dumbbell using formulas for volume of sphere and volume for cylinder
- Calculate the mass of the dumbbell in four metals: aluminum, iron, lead, and gold
- Use the density of the four metals in g/cm^3 to help calculate the mass of each dumbbell

USER INSTRUCTIONS:
- Follow the command line interface prompts
